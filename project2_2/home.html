Alexandre Dumas


Brief introduction: 1802 - 1870, a French romantic writer in the 19th century. Buried in Panthéon, France.

Representative works: "The Count of Monte Cristo", "Les Trois Mousquetaires"

Antoine de Saint-Exupéry


Brief introduction: 1900 - 1944, French writer and pilot, born in Lyon. In 1944, he wore armor against the Nazi German army. He disappeared during a flight mission on July 31, and was later awarded the title of "French Martyr".

Representative works: "Night Flight", "The Little Prince"

Zhou Shuren


Brief introduction: 1881 - 1936, he was famous in the world under the pseudonym Lu Xun, a native of Shaoxing, Zhejiang. He was a famous writer in modern China, one of the leaders of the New Culture Movement, the founder and pioneer of modern Chinese literature.

Representative works: "Scream", "Wandering"
